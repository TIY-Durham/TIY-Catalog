Policies

Payment

I hope you love my payment policy as much as I do: I accept Paypal or credit card via Paypal. You do not need a Paypal Account to pay for your items through Paypal. Choose the Paypal Option during checkout. After you submit your order click the "Pay now" button. Scroll down and you will see a credit or debit card without having to sign up for a Paypal account. Customers in the Netherlands can pay with a direct bank transfer if they wish, please contact me directly if you want to use this method.

Shipping

If your order is a gift going directly to the recipient, I can include a personal message card from you for no extra charge. If you would like this service please let me know what you would like me to write. All items are shipped in a dust bag and shipped in a sturdy giftwrap bubble envelope. We ship worldwide from the Netherlands using TNT post. I do not ship with tracking as standard as it is very expensive, if you wish to ship with tracking please contact me before ordering for a quote. DeLuxscarf is located in the Netherlands, Europe. - Shipping within the Netherlands normally takes 1 or 2 working days. - Packages shipped to Europe normally take 5-8 working days. - Shipping to the USA have been experiencing some delays due to American customs legislation. Packages normally take around 1 or 2 weeks to arrive, however sometimes it can take longer. This does not happen often, but it can happen. - Shipping to the rest of the world takes approximately 1-2 weeks, although customs can occasionally delay packages further.
